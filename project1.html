Real Estate CRM &
Lead Management System

A high-performance Salesforce architecture designed to synchronize property listings, automate lead scoring, and secure sensitive owner data for an integrated real estate ecosystem.

Project Overview

This project involved building a custom Real Estate CRM on Salesforce for a client who wanted to manage leads coming from an external application and track property listings and inquiries within Salesforce. The system ensures role-based access, automation, and a streamlined sales pipeline from lead capture to opportunity closure.

Business Problem

The client lacked a centralized CRM system to manage property inquiries, leads, and ownership details. They needed a secure way to integrate an external application, restrict sensitive property access to admins, and allow sales users to manage inquiries and sales activities efficiently.

Solution Delivered

  • • Integrated external app with Salesforce
  • • Built role-based access for Admin/Sales
  • • Automated lead-to-opportunity flow
  • • Custom UI for listings & inquiries
